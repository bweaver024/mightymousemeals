Korean Style Chicken

KOREAN STYLE CHICKEN RECIPE

Ingredients:

1½ lbs of chicken thighs or breasts (I used boneless)
½ cup brown rice
Zucchini mix
Gochujang sauce
Kimchi
Green onions for garnish
Sesame seeds for garnish
Marinade:
3 T low sodium soy sauce or Bragg liquid aminos
Sesame seeds for garnish
1 ½ T honey or agave
2 t sesame oil
½ T garlic (minced)
½ T ginger
1 T sriracha

Steps:
  1. 1. Preheat broiler to the highest setting.


  2. 2. Rinse brown rice.


  3. 3. Add 2 cups of water for every 1 cup of brown rice to rice cooker.


  4. 4. Combine the ingredients for the marinade. Set aside 1/4 cup of the marinade for basting the chicken while in the broiler. Then place remainder of marinade in a bag with chicken and marinate for at least 30 minutes.


  5. 5. Dice vegetables and toss in olive oil, black pepper, garlic salt and set aside.


  6. 6. Preheat saute pan on medium heat for the vegetables.


  7. 7. Once pan is hot, add oil.


  8. 8. Next, remove chicken from marinade and pat dry with a paper towel. Drying excess liquid will create a nice sear on the chicken.


  9. 9. Place chicken on foil covered pan and into the broiler 6 inches from the heat source


  10. 10. Once your oil is hot, add vegetables into the pan stirring frequently.


  11. 11. After 8 minutes flip the chicken, baste with extra marinade and broil for another 6-8 minutes.
