Jerk Chicken & Sweet Potatoes

Using a grill to meal prep adds flavor and texture that baked meats simply can't give you. Grills are generally large enough to prep your whole weeks worth of protein, however if you don't have a grill I am a firm believer of the broiler!

Greek Chicken with Vegetables and Spicy Tahini dip

Ingredients:

Chicken Marinade
2 lbs of Chicken Breast or thighs
1 T Honey
1 t Paprika
1 T Cumin
2 t of salt and pepper
Juice of 2 lemons
4 cloves of garlic
1t of crushed red pepper flakes
3T of olive oil

2 sweet potatoes
1 lbs of your favorite vegetables(I usually do asparugus)
1 t olive oil
salt and pepper to taste

Greek Yogurt Tahini sauce
1 cup of plain greek yogurt
2 T of spicy Tahini
2 cloves of minced garlic
Juice of 1 lemon
1T chopped mint

Steps:
  1. 1.Chop chicken into bite size pieces (approximate 1 inch).

  2. 2. Throw chicken and marinade ingredients in a bag and marinade for 30 minutes to a few hours (the longer the better)


  3. 3. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F

  4. 4. Trim the asaprugus and cube sweet potatoes

  5. 5. Once oven is preheated put sweet potatoes in the oven and bake for 20 minutes


  6. 6. After 20 minutes mix the sweet potatoes and put the asparus and chicken into the oven for another 15-20 minutes


  7. 7. While waiting for your chicken mix the ingredients for the Tahini dip. Remove once chicken reaches 160 degrees F.


    8. Eat and enjoy!
