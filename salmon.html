Super Simple Garlic Salmon

BAKED LEMON GARLIC SALMON RECIPE


Ingredients:

  • 1 or 2 6 oz Salmon pieces

  • 1 small clove of garlic for each 6oz salmon

  • Crown of Broccoli

  • 1 lemon

  • Parmesan Cheese for garnish

  • Pepper

  • Salt

  • 1 t olive oil


    •  Steps:
    1. 1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F


    2. 2. Cut salmon into 6-8 oz strips


    3. 3. Spread minced garlic on top of the salmon, crack fresh pepper,salt onto salmon and squeeze some lemon juice & olive oil over the salmon.


    4. 4. Trim the broccoli stems from the florets and toss them in a dash of olive oil and sprinkle some garlic salt and black pepper.


    5. 5.Toss that salmon and broccoli into the oven and bake for 8-12 minutes or until internal temperature of salmon reached 145 degrees F .
